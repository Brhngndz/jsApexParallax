APEX GUIDE

Apex Shared Abilities

Each Legend is defined by their unique Tactical ability, Passive ability, and Ultimate ability, the following abilities however are shared across the board, although some Legends may enhance one or more of these:

  • Each Legend is part of a class, which are categorized between Offensive, Defensive, Recon and Support.
  • Legends can walk, sprint, jump, crouch, crouch walk, slide, mantle, and climb walls up to 5-6 meters tall; Through the use of their jump kits, Legends can skydive from high altitudes, boost themselves along zip lines, and negate fall damage;
  • Legends can equip up to two firearms and one of each kind of gear; Legends have 10 inventory slots that can be used to carry ammo, ordnance, regen items, and spare attachments;
  • Legends can perform melee attacks at any time, which slightly knock back the enemy while also dealing damage;

Available Legends Classes

  • Offensive: Offensive Legends have abilities that are useful in combat.
  • Defensive: Defensive Legends can provide cover or fortify an area, making it safer for their squad.
  • Recon: Recon Legends can gather valuable intel, such as enemy whereabouts or the next ring location.
  • Support: Support Legends can aid their squad by providing healing or loot.

A WORLD WHERE ANYTHING GOES

Hitboxes

Legends come in three different sizes: large, medium, and small. Though each Legend's hitbox is a bit different, in general, Legends of the same size use the same hitbox "rig" in which their hitboxes are based off of. This means that Caustic's hitbox is the same size as Gibraltar's, Mirage's hitbox is the same size as Bangalore's, Octane's, Crypto's, etc. and Bloodhound has the same head hitbox as all the other medium Legends, despite their head looking bigger. Because hitbox sizes significantly impact gameplay, Fortified and Low Profile were added in an attempt to even the playing field. In Season Season 9, Low Profile was removed, causing the small Legends to receive boosts in win rate thanks to them being objectively smaller than other Legends with nothing to counteract it. In response, efforts were made to bring hitboxes closer together.
